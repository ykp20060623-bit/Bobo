Recycle. It’s that simple.

Our toothpaste tubes are now 100% recyclable. Help us close the loop.

1

Use it up

Squeeze every last bit of toothpaste. No need to rinse the inside—just finish the tube.

2

Rinse lightly

Give the tube a quick rinse under water to remove any remaining paste.

3

Recycle with cap on

Put the cap back on and toss the tube in your recycling bin. Both are recyclable.

Why it matters

Our tubes are designed to be 100% recyclable where facilities exist. By recycling, you help reduce plastic waste and support a circular economy.

Check local guidelines: Recycling programs vary. Some municipalities accept flexible plastics; others don’t. When in doubt, visit your local recycling center’s website.
